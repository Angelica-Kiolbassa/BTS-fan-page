Run BTS

These lyrics seem to reflect how the members of BTS turned to one another as they experienced ups and downs climbing from the bottom of a saturated musical landscape toward success at breakneck speed. Despite hardships the members were always there for one another, encouraging one another to push on. Ultimately Run BTS's lyrics are about the determination to never lose their spirit and avoid negativity with lines like, "Not losing our spirit," "Run bulletproof, run, yeah, you gotta run," and "With the ignorant faith, just run, with your two legs." After nine years, BTS has yet to lose the spirit they cultivated in that tiny dorm in Nonhyeon-dong, and as the lyrics of "Run BTS" say, they'll continue to run resiliently forward past any hurdles — like they're bulletproof.

Butter

"Butter" sees our Bangtan boys doing what they do best, i.e. making infectious pop music for their fans to dance to. And speaking of dance music, the lyrics to "Butter" actually reference two of pop's biggest icons: Michael Jackson and Usher. Quite literally the song opens with Jungkook singing, "Smooth like butter / Like a criminal undercover," a nod to Michael's 1988 hit, "Smooth Criminal." And by the time the second verse rolls around, V sings "Pull you in like no other / Don't need no Usher / To remind me you got it bad," which is inspired by Usher's 2001 hit "U Got It Bad." The overall theme is obvious though as our BTS boys are basically saying they are just SO charming, SO charismatic, and SO smooth that it makes people fall in love them and wanna dance.

Release

May 21, 2021

Take Two

BTS “Take Two” song Celebrates 10th Anniversary with New Single ‘Take Two’. A fan song filled with the heartfelt feelings of the seven members for their fans around the world SUGA is in charge of producing, with RM and J-Hope participating in songwriting. On June 9, 2023, in celebration of their 10th anniversary (debut date: June 13), BTS simultaneously released their new digital single ‘Take Two’ worldwide as a gift for their fans. ‘Take Two’ represents the second chapter in the journey of BTS. The song is themed around ‘Take Two’, reflecting on the days the band spent with their fans over the past 10 years and their expectations for the days to come. The heartfelt feelings of the seven members towards their fans (ARMY) are fully embodied in ‘Take Two’.
